Beat This Number


Test your skills and see if you can choose your way to the top! In this logic-based game, you will need to use the university school logo icons to generate a random value between 1-4. Clicking each option will reveal its value. Choose Wisely! Each click will add that icon's value to the current score. To win, correctly match your score to the game's random number. If you exceed the game's number, you lose! Let's see what you got!
